Ryu has four self-serve plans and one enterprise tier. The marketing pricing page at [ryuhq.com/pricing](https://ryuhq.com/pricing) shows the headline features; this page covers the enforced limits, credit pools, capability bands, and fine print.

<Callout type="info">
  The numbers on this page are the **enforced** limits from `packages/auth/src/lib/plans.ts`, not marketing copy. When the code and this page disagree, the code wins.
</Callout>

## Plans at a glance

| Plan | Price | Billing | AI credit pool | Deposit fee |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | — | — | — |
| **Lifetime** | $29 one-time (launch price) | Once | $0 | 10% |
| **Pro** | $39/mo ($390/yr) | Monthly or annual | $20/mo | 10% |
| **Max** | $200/mo ($2,000/yr) | Monthly or annual | $150/mo (75%) | 5% |
| **Teams** | $49/seat/mo ($490/seat/yr) | Monthly or annual | $24.50/seat/mo (50%) | 5% |

Annual billing gives **two months free** (pay for 10 of 12 months).

---

## Free (no plan)

The desktop shell and local/BYOK chat are **always free**. The paywall is dismissible — closing it drops you into free local chat. Free users get:

- Local and BYOK chat (unlimited)
- Single agent, tool calling, MCP, skills
- Basic run-while-open background tasks
- All the numeric caps listed in [Free-tier limits](/docs/billing/limits)

Free users **cannot** top up credits or use managed inference.

---

## Lifetime (desktop license)

A one-time $29 purchase (launch price, normally $69). A personal, single-device license.

**What you unlock:**

- All Band 2 ("Pro") features forever — council, background runs, prompt studio, fine-tuning, evals, graphrag, companion overlay, clips
- Unlimited agents, workflows, spaces, plugins, skills, schedules
- 3 concurrent background runs, 20 GB space storage
- One year of app updates included (after that, buy again to extend or stay on your last version)

**What you do NOT get (Band 3 — subscription only):**

- Managed inference (no cloud models — you bring your own keys)
- Cloud sync, cloud nodes, hosted bots, team seats
- Agent Inboxes
- Monthly credit pool

**Billing scope:** personal (`"user"`). No org association.

---

## Pro

$39/mo or $390/yr. The entry-level managed plan. **Strictly single-seat** — one person, one price.

**What you unlock over Lifetime:**

- **Managed inference** — 300+ cloud models via OpenRouter, no API keys needed
- **$20/mo included AI credits** — pooled, use-it-or-lose-it each billing period (not derived from the 50% rule; pinned to a round $20)
- **Agent Inboxes** — unlimited inboxes, 1,000 sends/mo, 5 GB storage, branding removable
- **Cloud sync** — sync state across devices
- Unlimited everything (agents, tabs, workflows, etc.)
- 3 concurrent background runs, 20 GB space storage
- 10% deposit fee on credit top-ups

**Billing scope:** personal (`"user"`). Cannot be used by multi-member orgs — if your org has 2+ members, you need Max or Teams.

**Seat model:** `{ kind: "single" }` — always 1, no scaling.

---

## Max

$200/mo or $2,000/yr. The flagship tier for power users and small teams who want 24/7 agents.

**What you unlock over Pro:**

- **$150/mo included AI credits** — 75% of the price (deliberately generous; the only plan that overrides the default 50% fraction)
- **Free managed cloud node** — 2 vCPU, 4 GB RAM, 40 GB SSD (the BASE tier, `cx23`). Auto-provisioned on subscription activation; auto-removed on cancellation. Your agents run 24/7 even when your computer is off.
- **Agent Inboxes** — unlimited inboxes, 25,000 sends/mo, 10 GB storage
- **5% deposit fee** on credit top-ups (half the base rate)
- **50 GB space storage** (vs Pro's 20 GB)
- **Seat-scalable** — buy N Max seats for a team (min 1, so solo buyers are unaffected)

**Billing scope:** org or personal. Max checkout supports a `seats` parameter for multi-seat orgs.

**Seat model:** `{ kind: "per_seat", minSeats: 1 }`. Billing and credit pool both scale per seat — 2 Max seats = $400/mo with $300/mo pooled credits.

### Max cloud node lifecycle

1. **Subscription activates** → webhook provisions a free `cx23` node (or reinstates one scheduled for removal)
2. **Subscription cancels** → node is scheduled for removal (grace period) and gateway tokens are revoked
3. **Larger instances** → purchased separately as ad-hoc cloud-instance subscriptions (dynamically priced from the Hetzner catalog with a 2.5x markup)

The free BASE node is flagged `includedWithMax: true` in the cloud catalog. It shows as "Included with Max" in the org dashboard — no checkout button.

---

## Teams

$49/seat/mo or $490/seat/yr. Minimum 2 seats. For organizations that want shared billing, pooled credits, and role-based access.

### What you unlock

- **$24.50/seat/mo AI credits** (50% of per-seat price), pooled across the entire org
- **Agent Inboxes** — unlimited inboxes, 100,000 sends/mo, 20 GB storage (org-wide, not per-seat)
- **8 concurrent background runs** (org parallelism — vs Pro/Max's 3)
- **50 GB space storage**
- **5% deposit fee** on top-ups
- **Channel users** — limit scales with seat count (1 channel user per seat)

### Billing scope

Teams is always **org-scoped** (`"org"`). Billing is charged under the canonical org billing email (the earliest owner's email). Only **owner** or **admin** members can manage billing.

### Seat model

`{ kind: "per_seat", minSeats: 2 }`. The seat count is validated server-side:

- Must be ≥ the org's live member count
- Must be ≥ 2 (the catalog minimum)
- Changing seats requires owner/admin role

The credit pool scales with seats: 5 seats = 5 × $24.50 = $122.50/mo pooled.

### RBAC (role-based access control)

Teams has a full permission system with four built-in roles:

| Role | Billing | Members | Config | Runs | Data |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Owner** | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full |
| **Admin** | — | Full | Full | Full | Full |
| **Member** | — | — | View | Run | Read/Write |
| **Viewer** | — | — | View | — | Read |

**Custom roles** are also supported — you can create roles with any combination of 17 granular permissions:

`gateway.view`, `gateway.configure`, `workflow.view`, `workflow.run`, `workflow.edit`, `workflow.delete`, `space.read`, `space.write`, `space.delete`, `agent.view`, `agent.run`, `agent.edit`, `tool.exec`, `members.manage`, `roles.manage`, `billing.manage`, `audit.view`

### Shared wallet

Teams has a **pooled org wallet** — every member draws from the same balance:

- Monthly plan credits are injected per billing period (use-it-or-lose-it)
- Top-up credits roll over and never expire
- All members can see the balance; only owner/admin can top up or change auto-recharge
- The wallet is visible in **Settings → Billing** (desktop) and **Organizations → Billing** (web)

### Org dashboard

Teams gets an aggregation dashboard at `/organizations/[orgId]/dashboard`:

- Eval leaderboard across all agents
- Shared budgets and spend tracking
- Audit table of agent actions
- Request/error/cost/token stats across all gateways

### Teams management

The org admin can:

- **Invite members** by email with a role (admin/member)
- **Change member roles** (owner/admin/member/viewer dropdown)
- **Create teams** (groups of org members, not to be confused with the "Teams" plan name)
- **Manage seats** — adjust billed seat count, see over-allocation alerts
- **Config-as-code** — org-wide gateway config with revision history and rollback
- **Policies** — locked guardrails, approved models, allowed regions

### What Teams does NOT include

- **No free cloud node** — unlike Max, Teams does not bundle a free `cx23` node. Cloud nodes are purchased separately.
- **No `team_workspace` feature** — that's the agent-team orchestration feature (groups of agents with coordination strategies), which is a separate concept from the Teams billing plan.

## Capability bands

Features are gated in three bands. The rule: runs on the user's machine at zero marginal cost → Band 1/2; Ryu pays a recurring bill → Band 3.

| Band | Name | Unlocked by | Features |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | Free | Always | Local/BYOK chat, agents, tool calling, MCP, skills, basic runs |
| **2** | Pro | Subscription, license key, OR trial | Council, local background runs, gateway governance UI, prompt studio, fine-tuning, evals, graphrag, companion overlay, clips |
| **3** | Subscription | Active recurring plan only | Managed inference, cloud sync, cloud node, hosted bots, team seats, Agent Inboxes |

<Callout type="warn">
  Lifetime license holders get Band 2 but **not** Band 3. Band 3 features cost Ryu money every month, so they require an active subscription.
</Callout>

## Deposit fees (credit top-ups)

Credits are prepaid — you spend your balance down as you go. Models are metered **at cost** (no per-token markup). A one-time deposit fee applies on each top-up:

| Plan | Fee rate | Minimum |
|---|---|---|
| Free | Cannot top up | — |
| Lifetime / Pro | 10% | $1.00 |
| Max / Teams | 5% | $1.00 |

The fee is the **greater of** the percentage and the $1.00 floor. Below $10 the floor dominates (a $5 top-up pays $1 ≈ 20% effective); above $10 the flat percentage takes over.
