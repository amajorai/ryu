Agents are powerful. Learning to wield them shouldn't be. **Ryu Academy** is a set of guided
courses that take you from opening the app for the first time to building and shipping your own
agents on the platform.

Every course is made of short lessons. Each lesson opens with what you will learn, walks through
the feature with real steps, and closes with a **knowledge check** so you can confirm it stuck.
Work through a track end to end, or jump straight to the lesson you need.

The number on each course is its **level**: `100` is the gentlest starting point, and
each `+100` step assumes a bit more of what came before.

## How the Academy works

- **Tracks by role** - Each course targets one kind of person. Start with Essentials, then follow
  the track that matches what you want to do.
- **Lessons, not walls of text** - A lesson is one idea, done well, in a few minutes.
- **Knowledge checks** - Every lesson ends with a short self-test. No grading, no login, just a
  way to be honest with yourself before moving on.
- **Grounded in the real app** - Nothing here is aspirational. Every step maps to a surface you can
  open in Ryu today, with deep links into the [reference docs](/docs/start-here) when you want more.

## Get certified

When you have worked through a track, the [certifications guide](/docs/academy/certifications)
lays out the four certification levels, what each one expects you to know, and how to prepare.

<Callout type="info">
  New to Ryu entirely? Begin with [Ryu Essentials](/docs/academy/essentials). It assumes nothing
