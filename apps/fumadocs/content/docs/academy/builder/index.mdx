Ryu is a control layer around any agent engine. This track is for developers who want to extend that platform: build agents, workflows, tools, and skills, bundle them into installable apps, and publish them to the marketplace.

You will learn one core idea (the Runnable contract) and then everything else composes on top of it.

## Who this is for

- Developers who want to ship agents, tools, or workflows on Ryu.
- People building plugins or apps to share with others.
- Anyone who needs every model call governed by the Gateway without wiring it by hand.

## What you will learn

- The Runnable contract and why five things share one shape.
- How to scaffold and run a project with the Ryu SDK.
- How to bundle Runnables into an installable app (plugin).
- How to publish that app to the federated marketplace.
