The desktop app is the primary surface for Ryu and the most complete one. This realm is the full feature reference: everything you do in the app, plus the desktop companions that ride alongside it (the Island overlay, the browser extension, and Raycast). The [CLI](/docs/cli) and [Mobile](/docs/mobile) realms cover those surfaces and how they differ.

Every surface talks to the **same Core** (the local backend on `:7980`), so a chat you start here is the same conversation you open in the CLI or on your phone.
