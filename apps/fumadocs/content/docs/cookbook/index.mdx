The Cookbook is practical, start-to-finish recipes. Each one takes a real goal, "watch a price and
ping Slack", "give a model a tool it can call", "route coding to Claude and chat to a local model",
and shows the exact features that get you there, linking the canonical docs for depth. Nothing here
is hypothetical: every step uses a capability Ryu ships today.

your own docs, and let recordings and coordinators do the work.

## Have one to add?

These are the kinds of flows that compose well: a [monitor](/docs/core/website-monitoring) that feeds
a [webhook](/docs/gateway/channels), a [workflow](/docs/core/workflows) that calls a
[delegate fan-out](/docs/core/delegation), a [coordinator](/docs/core/coordinator-threads) that drives
a fleet of worker threads, or an [SDK Runnable](/docs/develop/sdk) whose every call routes through the
[gateway](/docs/gateway). Mix the building blocks in the [Core](/docs/core),
[Using Ryu](/docs/using-ryu), and [Develop](/docs/develop) sections to make your own.
